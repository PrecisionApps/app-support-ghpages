Frequently Asked Questions

How does the water ejection work?

Eject The Water uses low-frequency sound waves (165Hz) to vibrate and push water out of your device's speaker grills. Simply tap the eject button and let the app play the sound sequence for 30-60 seconds. Make sure your volume is turned up for best results.

Is it safe for my device?

Yes, completely safe! The app uses the same frequency range as Apple Watch's built-in water ejection feature. It won't damage your speakers or device. The sound is designed specifically for this purpose.

My device still has water after using the app

Try running the ejection sequence 2-3 times. Gently shake your device with the speaker facing down between cycles. For stubborn water, let your device air dry in a well-ventilated area for a few hours.

Yes! Eject The Water works on all iOS devices with speakers (iPhone, iPad). It's most effective on devices with stereo speakers. Results may vary based on how much water is present and device orientation.
